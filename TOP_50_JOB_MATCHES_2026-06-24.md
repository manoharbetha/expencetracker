## Application strategy

1. Apply to ranks 1–15 first, ideally within 24 hours.
2. Use five resume variants: **AI Automation**, **GenAI/RAG**, **Full-Stack AI**, **Python Backend**, and **ML**.
3. Put the most relevant proof in the first two bullets. For example, lead CV3 with the n8n RAG workflow; lead Megaminds with Fintell plus the RAG assistant.
4. For LinkedIn roles showing 200+ applicants, seek a recruiter/employee referral before or immediately after applying.
5. Add a short application note: one sentence on stack overlap, one sentence on a shipped project, one measurable result, and the GitHub/demo link.
6. Apply in two waves: top 25 now; remaining 25 after checking which applications produce responses and refining the pitch.

The highest interview probability is concentrated in ranks **1–25**, especially small-company roles that explicitly value shipped projects and students rather than years of employment.
